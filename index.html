About Teacher's Toolkit App

Overview

Teacher's Toolkit App is designed to help Kenyan teachers easily manage their professional responsibilities through quick access to essential Teachers Service Commission (TSC) systems, including the Teachers Performance Appraisal and Development (TPAD), Leave Application, and Transfer Application portals. By streamlining these processes, TSC Tpad allows teachers to stay compliant with TSC requirements efficiently.

Features

  • Access the TPAD system to set targets and complete self-appraisals.
  • Submit and manage leave applications through the Leave Application Portal.
  • Request and track transfer applications with ease.
  • Receive notifications to stay on top of appraisal deadlines and updates.

Developer

Cyrocom Technologies is an independent development initiative aimed at simplifying processes for teachers in Kenya. The app was developed by a teacher with the goal of supporting educators in their day-to-day administrative responsibilities.

Disclaimer

This app, Teacher's Toolkit, is an independent service developed by Cyrocom Technologies. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the Teachers Service Commission (TSC). All links to the TPAD system, Leave Application Portal, and Transfer Application Portal lead to official TSC websites. Any data entered through these systems is handled directly by the TSC. The app serves only as a platform to facilitate easy access to these systems.
